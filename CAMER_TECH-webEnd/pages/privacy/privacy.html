Camer Tech Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 04-09-2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:
We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and physical address.

Device Information:
Details about the devices you register for repair.

Payment Information:
Payment details for processing transactions securely.

Ratings and Feedback:
Information you provide after service is rendered.

Usage Data:
Data on how you interact with our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

To connect you with a technician, facilitate the repair process, and ensure secure payment.

For communication, sending updates, and providing customer support.

To monitor the quality of our technicians and improve the platform.

For security purposes in compliance with Cameroonian law.

3. Sharing Your Information

With Technicians: Relevant information shared with the assigned technician.

Third-Party Services: Used for payment processing and analytics under confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements: Disclosed if required by Cameroonian law.

4. Your Rights and Choices

Access and update personal information through your account settings.

Rate your technician and provide feedback.

Opt-out of promotional emails and notifications.

5. Data Security

We implement various security measures in compliance with Cameroonian law but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy and will notify you of any changes by posting the new version on our website.
